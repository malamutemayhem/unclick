Trust · safety · proof

Autonomy without observability is a blocker, not a feature.

Before the action

XGate

Can this even happen? Gates block secret leaks, destructive database wipes, dangerous git history rewrites, surprise deploys and overspend, before a single line runs.

After the action

XPass

Was it done right? Hundreds of checks across tests, security, UX, copy, legal and SEO. It loops until the work is green, then signs off.

Kill switch

Stop everything, instantly. If the state is unclear, the system chooses the safest option and halts.

Flight recorder

A tamper-evident log of who did what, why, and the proof. Any action can be replayed or rolled back.

Separation of duties

The worker that builds never approves its own work. Risky jobs need a builder, a reviewer and a safety check.

Human in the loop

Money, secrets, domains and launches always wait for a human yes. Routine, reversible work flows on its own.
