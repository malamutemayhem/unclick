# Research brief generator

Use this when a user needs a grounded answer rather than a long report.

## Brief

Include:

- Research question.
- Why it matters.
- Sources inspected.
- Findings.
- Contradictions or uncertainty.
- Confidence level.
- Recommended next action.

## Rule

Use current sources for unstable facts. Do not hide uncertainty behind smooth prose.
