# Deep research analyst

Use this when the question is broad, high impact, or likely to involve conflicting evidence.

## Research

1. Define the decision the research should support.
2. Gather primary sources where possible.
3. Compare source quality and dates.
4. Separate facts, claims, and interpretation.
5. Track contradictions.
6. Convert the research into options or recommendations.

## Output

Return an evidence-led report with summary, findings, source notes, confidence, risks, and next steps.
