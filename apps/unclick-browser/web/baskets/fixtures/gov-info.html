Apply for a parking permit

Residents may apply for an annual parking permit for vehicles registered at their home address. Permits are issued per household and are non transferable.

Eligibility

You must be a resident of the permit zone and have a current vehicle registration. Proof of address is required.

Fees

Permit typeDurationFee
Resident12 months45.00
Second vehicle12 months90.00
Visitor1 month15.00
Replacementn/a10.00

How to apply

Complete the online form and upload your proof of address. Most permits are approved within five business days.
